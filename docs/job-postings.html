Senior Middleware Architect

Transform Financial Access for 30 Million Underbanked Americans

Location: West Palm Beach, FL (Hybrid) or Remote

Compensation: $150,000 - $175,000 base + equity + comprehensive benefits

Experience: 8-12 years backend, 5+ years Django

About MyBambu

MyBambu is the leading digital financial platform serving Latino immigrants in the United States. We provide essential banking services to 20-30 million underbanked users who manage over $170 billion in annual remittances. Our mission is simple yet powerful: democratize access to financial services for those who need it most.

The Opportunity

We're seeking a Senior Middleware Architect to lead the transformation of our Django monolith into a scalable microservices architecture. This is a rare opportunity to architect systems that directly impact millions of hardworking families while solving complex technical challenges at scale.

You'll join at a pivotal moment as we scale from our current 30M user base to 50M+ over the next two years. Your architectural decisions will shape how millions of people send money home, pay bills, and build financial stability in America.

What You'll Do

  • Lead Architectural Transformation: Decompose our 52-app Django monolith into well-designed microservices while maintaining 99.9% uptime
  • Optimize Critical Systems: Eliminate 346 identified N+1 queries and reduce response times from 3-5 seconds to under 500ms
  • Design Integration Architecture: Architect resilient integration patterns for 22 third-party services including Galileo, Plaid, TabaPay, and MoneyGram
  • Scale Data Infrastructure: Optimize PostgreSQL performance handling 50,000+ daily transactions and implement intelligent caching strategies
  • Modernize Deployment Pipeline: Reduce deployment cycles from 2 weeks to 2 days through CI/CD improvements and service isolation
  • Mentor Engineering Teams: Guide 10+ engineers through the transformation, establishing best practices and architectural patterns
  • Drive Technical Strategy: Partner with leadership to define and execute the 3-year technical roadmap

What You Bring

Required Experience:

  • 8-12 years of backend development with 5+ years of Django production experience
  • Successfully led 2+ monolith-to-microservices transformations at scale
  • Deep expertise in PostgreSQL optimization, including query planning and index strategies
  • Proven experience with distributed systems, message queues (Celery), and caching (Redis)
  • Strong API design skills (REST, GraphQL, gRPC) with versioning and backward compatibility
  • Experience with containerization (Docker, Kubernetes) and cloud platforms (AWS preferred)

Preferred Qualifications:

  • Financial services or fintech experience, especially in payments or banking
  • Experience with high-compliance environments (PCI-DSS, SOC2)
  • Knowledge of event-driven architectures and domain-driven design
  • Familiarity with modern observability tools (DataDog, New Relic)
  • Contributions to open source projects or technical thought leadership

Technical Environment

  • Backend: Django 4.2.9, Django REST Framework, Python 3.x
  • Data: PostgreSQL, Redis, Celery with 15+ specialized queues
  • Integrations: 22 third-party APIs including banking, payments, and verification services
  • Infrastructure: AWS, Docker, GitHub Actions
  • Scale: 884 API endpoints, 50K+ daily transactions, 30M+ users

What We Offer

  • Competitive Compensation: $150K-$175K base salary based on experience
  • Equity Participation: Meaningful equity stake in a high-growth fintech
  • Comprehensive Benefits: Medical, dental, vision, 401(k) with matching
  • Work-Life Balance: Flexible hybrid/remote options, unlimited PTO
  • Growth Opportunities: Direct path to Principal/Staff Engineer or Engineering Director
  • Impactful Mission: Your work directly improves financial access for millions

Our Values

  • Inclusion First: We build for those traditionally excluded from financial services
  • Technical Excellence: We solve hard problems with elegant, scalable solutions
  • Customer Obsession: Every line of code impacts real families and communities
  • Continuous Learning: We invest in our team's growth and development

Interview Process

We respect your time and move quickly:

  • Week 1: Initial recruiter screen (30 min) + Technical phone screen (60 min)
  • Week 2: Take-home challenge (4-6 hours) focused on service extraction
  • Week 3: Virtual onsite: Architecture (90 min), Technical (60 min), Leadership (45 min)
  • Week 4: Executive conversation + Reference checks + Offer

Ready to transform financial access for millions? Apply with your resume and a brief note about the most challenging architectural transformation you've led. We're excited to learn about your experience decomposing monoliths, optimizing databases, and building resilient distributed systems.
